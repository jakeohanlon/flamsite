Our Services

Welcome home to exquisite elegance with Golden Dragon Carpets & Rugs by PT. Flamindo Carpetama. Established since 1992, PT. Flamindo Carpetama is proud to be one of the leading carpet manufactures, distributor and exporter in the Indonesian rug industry, specializing in the hand tufting process. Our carpets have graced a variety of interiors, from palaces to hotels, restaurants to commercial buildings, even yachts and entertainment centers. We offer our customers an outstanding experience through exceptional products and unforgettable service.
